## Frequently Asked Questions

**Difference between legal consultants and advocates?**
Legal consultants focus on advisory, risk analysis, and document preparation. Advocates provide these services plus court representation.

**Legal due diligence timeline?**
Typically 2-3 weeks for SMEs; 4-8 weeks for larger corporations, with interim reports provided.

**Online consultation available?**
Yes, via video call, email, and WhatsApp with digital document handling and electronic signatures.

**Fee structure?**
Monthly retainer for companies, project-based fees for specific transactions, and hourly rates for ad-hoc consultation.
