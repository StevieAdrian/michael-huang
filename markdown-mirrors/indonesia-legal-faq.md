# Indonesia Legal FAQ

Frequently asked questions regarding legal services, notary, company establishment, intellectual property, and business consulting in Indonesia.

## Layanan hukum apa saja yang Anda sediakan?

Comprehensive legal support for businesses and individuals, including corporate matters (PT/CV establishment, contract drafting), M&A with due diligence, and regulatory compliance with BKPM, OJK, and KPPN.

## Bagaimana tahapan mendirikan perusahaan PT atau PMA?

Process begins with notarized founding deed covering capital structure and business classification (KBLI), followed by registration with the Ministry of Law and Human Rights, then NIB acquisition through OSS for operational permits and NPWP. Foreign ownership restrictions apply to PMA. Typical timeline: 2-4 weeks.

## Berapa biayanya untuk mendapatkan konsultasi dan pengurusan legal?

Costs vary by complexity. Standard consultation starts at accessible rates; PT establishment includes notary fees at competitive rates per regulations. Acquisition support scales with transaction value. Fixed-fee packages available for startups/SMEs; monthly retainer options for ongoing corporate services.

## Dokumen apa saja yang diurus oleh Notaris & PPAT?

Authentic documents including property sales deeds (AJB), mortgage rights deeds (APHT), property certificate verification (SHM), powers of attorney, company founding deeds, inheritance matters, and electronic deeds per applicable standards.

## Apakah proses notaris sudah dapat diproses secara elektronik/digital?

Tax validation (BPHTB, PPh) and OSS licensing are digitized for efficiency. However, physical notary signatures and party presence remain mandatory requirements for document authenticity and fraud prevention.

## Berapa lama estimasi waktu pengurusan Akta Jual Beli (AJB) Properti?

Estimated 1-3 business days after complete document verification. PPAT fees follow regional standards as percentage of transaction value. BPN name transfer processes have accelerated with recent service improvements.

## Apa perizinan hukum esensial untuk membuka bisnis kuliner UMKM?

NIB registration via OSS and location environmental permits required. Depending on scale, food safety permits (P-IRT) and Halal certification may be necessary. Franchise operations require formal franchise agreements under investment protection law.

## Bagaimana saran mengatur kontrak lokasi usaha komersial properti?

Commercial lease agreements should clearly specify food business use, appropriate lease duration relative to investment recovery, and building renovation rights to prevent disputes with property owners.

## Adakah standar hukum dalam mempekerjakan karyawan?

Fixed-term employment agreements (PKWT) should clearly define rights, obligations, and working hours specific to F&B industry standards, protecting both employer and employee while maintaining operational efficiency.

## Apa saja langkah legal teknis untuk mengubah properti menjadi unit bisnis Kost?

Verify building permits (IMB/PBG) for residential use. Obtain NIB for accommodation sector to avoid local tax violations and property seizure risks.

## Seberapa pentingnya Aturan Penghuni dan dokumen Perjanjian Sewa untuk Kost?

Written tenancy agreements are essential legal tools. Should specify payment terms, subletting prohibitions, guest limits, damage deposit policies, and termination rights to prevent problematic tenants.

## Apa kewajiban perlindungan dan keamanan fasilitas premium terhadap penyewa Kost?

Tenant privacy and property security must be guaranteed. 24-hour CCTV monitoring recommended for common areas only, while respecting privacy in hallways and individual rooms.
